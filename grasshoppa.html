Grasshoppa

Next

This project reimagines Grasshoppa! Films, a production house known for its experimental, handcrafted filmmaking methods. The redesign captures this spirit through a paper–cut typographic treatment and a shifting type system paired with a diamond motif referencing keyframes, evoking both the hop of a grasshopper and the frame–by–frame rhythm of animation.
