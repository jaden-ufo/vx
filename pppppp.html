PPP, PPP!

Next

PPP, PPP! is a surreal survival game about the Cherokee Wax Bean Plant—a harmless legume that grows stronger the more it’s attacked. Tumbling letterforms set against strict visual rules, and the interplay between a spiky serif and geometric sans, mirror the plant’s transformation from benign to unhinged.
