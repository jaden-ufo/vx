Poster Series, Data Visualization

O(Life) is an experimental data visualization poster series that uses Big–O notation—normally used to describe algorithm efficiency—to map the growth and frequency of five key aspects of my life. Each poster visualizes a life category through gradients and rings that represent how these experiences have evolved over time, based on their assigned functions.

March 2025
Mentored by Meredith Zerby, Anastasia Kharchenko, & Renee Freiha
